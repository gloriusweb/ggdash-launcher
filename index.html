FAQ

Q: I did not receive an account activation link on gmail.

A: After registering an account, the letter should not come to your e-mail. You need to go to https://gofruit.space/gdps/001X and log in to your gdps account there. Then log in to the game.

Q: How to add music?

A: To add music use this site:

https://gofruit.space/gdps/001X

Q: Why didn't my level get a rate?

A: Please check our discord server's criteria. If your level meets all the requirements, then send it to us.

Q: Why can't I post the level?

A: Check if you are registered on gdps, if your level has 100 objects, if you have collected all coins during the level, and if you have passed the level at all.

Q: I get error 00xc0000142 when running gdps

A: Throw the folder with gdps on disk C

Q: Can I download on gdps cheats?

A: You can, but on the condition that everything will be used for reasonable purposes
